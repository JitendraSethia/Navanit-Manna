## 🎯 Fastest Method: Netlify Drop

### Step-by-Step (Literally 2 Minutes):

1. **Open Netlify Drop**
   - Go to: https://app.netlify.com/drop
   - No account needed!

2. **Drag Your Folder**
   - Find the "N Manna" folder on your desktop
   - Drag the ENTIRE folder into the Netlify Drop page
   - Wait 30 seconds...

3. **Done! 🎉**
   - You'll get a link like: `https://random-name-123.netlify.app`
   - Your website is LIVE!
   - Share this link with anyone

1. Create free Netlify account (30 seconds)
2. Click "Claim this site"
3. Change name to: `dr-n-manna-clinic`
4. New URL: `https://dr-n-manna-clinic.netlify.app`
