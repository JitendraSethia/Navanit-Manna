About the Doctor

Meet Dr. N Manna

Dr. N Manna is an experienced dermatologist and trichologist based in Rishra, West Bengal. With a thorough consultation process and a genuine focus on understanding the root cause of each patient's concern, Dr. Manna has built a reputation for delivering real, lasting results.

The clinic specialises in advanced skin and hair treatments including Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT) for hair regrowth, acne management, pigmentation correction, and comprehensive hair care — all in a welcoming, inclusive environment.
